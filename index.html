About Us

Avicenna Tutorial Kharichala

Avicenna Tutorial Kharichala is a trusted educational establishment located in Haldhia Pather, Jamer Kur, Assam. Named after the legendary scholar Avicenna, our tutorial center is dedicated to nurturing young minds with strong academic foundations, personalized attention, and a passion for learning. We serve students from the local community with commitment, care, and a results-driven approach to education.

Our doors are open six days a week from early morning, reflecting our dedication to flexible and accessible learning for every student. Whether your child needs support in core subjects or guidance to excel in competitive academics, our experienced tutors are here to help. At Avicenna Tutorial Kharichala, we believe every student has the potential to succeed — and we are here to help them realize it.

  • Open 6 days a week from 5:30 AM for early morning study sessions
  • Personalized attention and small batch sizes for effective learning
  • Experienced and dedicated tutors committed to student success
